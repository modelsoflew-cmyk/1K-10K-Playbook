The optimal combination for freelancers, traders & remote workers

1PayoneerPrimary account
2Trust WalletNo-KYC crypto storage
3Binance P2PConvert USDT to LKR
4Western UnionFamily remittance

Important: PayPal & Stripe don't officially support receiving money in Sri Lanka due to central bank regulations. Payoneer or Skrill remain the go-to alternatives.

Crypto Safety Note: Non-custodial wallets (Trust Wallet, MetaMask, etc.) give you full control — but also full responsibility. Never share your seed phrase. If you lose it, funds are unrecoverable. No-KYC platforms carry counterparty risk — always use escrow-protected P2P trades.
